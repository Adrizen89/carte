EXTRA PALE UIL

REAL PALE ALE

Aroma Passion Fruit and Guava
Flavour Light Malty characters
Bitterness 46/100 IBU's

With an ABV of 3.8% and a dry, clean finish, this session beer suits thirsty summer afternoons in the Sun.
When the opportunity for a pun or play on words comes along we can never pass it up! So for those who do not originate from the shores of South Africa, an Uil (pronounced ale) is the Afrikaans translationof Owl.
